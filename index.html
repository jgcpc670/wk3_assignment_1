Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you must always experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:

     1. Loria Restaurant
     2. Everest Kitchen
     3. Angry Penne
     4. Naked Fish
     5. Wild Bills

Loria Restaurant | San Roque, Saipan

Loria Restaurant serves a great buffet with a variety of cuisines, from Asian to American. The quality is unlike any other buffet on the island. It is located in the Kensington Hotel, where you can enjoy beatuiful views as well.

    What I love:
    • Their Potato Au Gratin
    • Desserts
    • Breathtaking views
